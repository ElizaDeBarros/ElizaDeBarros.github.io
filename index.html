About me...

Born and raised in Recife, Brazil, I come from humble beginnings. My mother worked two jobs, and my father worked tirelessly to support my brother and I, ensuring we could attend a middle-class private school since the public education system in Brazil is severely lacking. Their sacrifices paved the way for my education and career, shaping the person I am today. In 2005, I moved to the USA to help Gamesa Energy to establish their Wind Resource Department in the Philadelphia office. With a strong background in Mechanical Engineering, I hold both a Bachelor's and Master’s degree from the Federal University of Pernambuco, Brazil.


I am a Senior Wind Resource Analyst with extensive experience in renewable energy projects. Over the years, I have been part of teams that developed and built more than 1.8 GW of wind energy projects, contributing to a more sustainable future.


Beyond my work in renewable energy, I am also an aspiring Data Scientist, having earned a certificate in Data Analysis from the University of Oregon. I am passionate about leveraging data-driven insights.


When I am not analyzing wind resources or diving into data, you will find me enjoying my family, my dogs, enjoying chocolate, relaxing at the beach or using my Nikon D90 to capture interesting moments. I practice hot yoga and kickboxing, which help me stay balanced and energized.
